Typography

Using a consistent, recognizable typography helps our customers identify our brand as we share our baked goods across the country. By following the standards, we are ensuring clear, impactful messaging for our customer base no matter where they may find themselves on our website, social media, or in our flagship bakery.

Helvetica

Helvetica is our most notable font. The neutrality and the impact it makes is what Hillcrest is about.
