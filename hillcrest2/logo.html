Primary Logo

The Hillcrest Little Bakery logo consists of HILLCREST LITTLE BAKERY and 1 rolling pin.

The logo should be the primary presentation of the brand.

Use of any stylized, animated, hand drawn, or other version of an official logo is not permitted. This undermines the logo system and brand consistency. Please consult with Hillcrest Little Bakery if you have any questions or need further help.

Alternate Logo

The alternative logo includes HLB, 2 rolling pins, and tagline: EAT LOCAL, THINK GLOBAL.

Examples of where this version of the logo can be used: website banners, small ads with limited space, social media icon, etc.

This brand expression guide should be used in conjunction with other more specific guides around each element of our brand.
