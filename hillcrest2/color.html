Our Main Three

White

By using the color white, at HLB we communicate goodness, clarity, energy and cleanliness. We embrace the color white, looking at it as an opportunity, not just space.

Teal

Our brand thrives off of the color teal. Teal is the calmness of blue, and the sustainability of green to create a shade that makes HLB. Teal has a natural dignity to it and isn't contrived or confrontational. People who are attracted to teal are authentic, genuine, creative and focused.

Black

Black signifies power and professionalism for us. As a family-owned business, black is used to embrace the strength in family. Black is a good neutral staple for our brand.
